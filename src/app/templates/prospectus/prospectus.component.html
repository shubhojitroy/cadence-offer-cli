Cadence Capital Limited (ASX:CDM)

Cadence Capital Limited is a Listed Investment Company (LIC) that invests in listed equities, with the aim of producing above market risk-adjusted returns over time. CDM listed on the ASX in December 2006 and currently has over $400m of Funds Under Management. 50% of our shareholders are Self Managed Super Funds (SMSF).
You can invest in CDM by buying shares on the ASX (ASX:CDM).
