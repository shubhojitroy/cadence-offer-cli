Frequently asked questions

The Offer is available to applicants with a registered address in Australia and New Zealand.

Applications under the General Offer or Broker Firm Offer must be for a minimum of $ value which is equal to X Units (at the Issue Price of $X per Units). A larger number of Units may be applied for in multiples of XX shares.

The minimum subscription amount payable by an Applicant under the Entitlement Offer is the Issue Price (of $X per Units) multiplied by the lower of:

  • X Units; or
  • the number of Units being offered to the Applicant (being an Eligible Shareholder under the Entitlement Offer).
